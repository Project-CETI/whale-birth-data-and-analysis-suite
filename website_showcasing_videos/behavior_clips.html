Rebuttal Video 1 (R1): Lifting the Newborn

Source video: 1688831374924

Rounder (the mother) supporting the newborn from below and Accra (the newborn’s half-sister) ahead of it with her mouth open. Soursop, an adult female from the other matriline, flanks the newborn (on its right side), as her calf, Ariel, ducks her nose under the newborn to lift it onto their backs.
Note: The umbilical cord is still attached, and the fetal folds along the peduncle of the newborn are visible at 7 seconds into the clip.
Approximately 5 minutes after delivery.

Source video: 1688831830406

The newborn swims out in front of the rest of the social unit as several females nose it from behind including its mother, Rounder.
